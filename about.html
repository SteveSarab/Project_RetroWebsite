Steve's Arcade was created on May 4, 2021, developed by Stephen Sarabdial. The website was created for players to relive the classic era of videogames. These would range from Nintendo Entertainment System to the Nintendo 64. The current games on this website run on a open-source emulator, known as JSNES, which was developed by Ben Firshman, or bfirsh on GitHub. You can check out the emulator yourself, by clicking this link.
